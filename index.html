nyc coding club
Photo by Roberto Nickson

Kimu is a community based (but not limited to) NYC composed of people who like coding things in their free time.

Game dev, web dev, app dev, backend, data science, etc is welcome

Entrepreneurs, open source contributors, academics, freelancers... all are welcome

Even other disciplines like art, film, music, etc are welcome albeit the core of the club will be software.

Natives (new yorkers) and Expats (non new yorkers) all are welcome albeit meetups will likely be in person in NYC.
